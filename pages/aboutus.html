ABOUT PRODIKPI

PRODIKPI is a national initiative to enhance the quality and relevance of vocational education in banking and finance.

  • Promote standardized yet flexible curricula
  • Support professional certifications
  • Strengthen collaborations in research, community service, and industry engagement
  • Build networks between member institutions while driving data-driven reforms
  • Helping provide diploma programs in the finance sector
  • Career-ready options in the financial sector

Our History

The Association of Indonesian Banking and Finance Diploma Programs (PRODIKPI) was established out of a shared need to improve the quality and relevance of vocational education in the financial sector. The rapid development of the financial industry, coupled with increasingly complex market demands, requires graduates who are not only theoretically proficient but also ready to work and adaptive to change.

The Beginning

The idea for PRODIKPI emerged from a series of informal discussions among several leaders of Finance and Banking Diploma programs from various universities in Indonesia. They recognized the importance of a unified platform to address common challenges, such as curriculum standardization, lecturer competency improvement, and strengthening links with the industry.

Our Structure

PRODIKPI is built on a solid organizational structure designed to effectively and efficiently achieve its vision and mission. This structure is composed of a board of advisors, a central executive board, and various commissions responsible for specific program areas. All members have the right to participate in decision-making through the annual National Conference.
